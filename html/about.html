Welcome to the Grinnell Artificial Intelligence and Ethics Showcase, or GAInES! This app can teach you about how copyright law and ethics are linked to artificial intelligence in complicated ways. The app is set up as a museum. To get started, click on the museum tab and click on a painting to enter a room. Clicking on the paintings inside bring up information to read. Some exhibitions will have other media, such as videos or music, which you can click on to play. Enjoy!
